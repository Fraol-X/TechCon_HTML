History

A gathering of tech enthusiasts led to the founding of TechCon in the 2000s. With thousands of guests from all around the world, it has developed into one of the most anticipated tech conferences throughout the years.

Mission

Our goal is to bring together the most brilliant minds in technology to promote learning, creativity, and teamwork. Our goal is to establish a welcoming atmosphere where people of all backgrounds can exchange ideas and information.
